Sinovac




CoronaVac

CoronaVac, also known as the Sinovac COVID-19 vaccine, is an inactivated virus COVID-19 vaccine developed by the Chinese company Sinovac Biotech. It has been in Phase III clinical trials in Brazil, Chile, Indonesia, the Philippines, and Turkey.

Side Effects of Vaccine

  • Fever
  • Fatigue
  • Headaches
  • Body aches
  • Chills
  • Nausea

Facts About Sinovac Vaccine

It can be stored in a standard refrigerator at 2-8 degrees Celsius

CoronaVac has been undergoing phase three trials in various countries. Interim data from late-stage trials in Turkey and Indonesia showed that the vaccine was 91.25% and 65.3% effective respectively.

Researchers in Brazil initially said it was 78% effective in their clinical trials, but in January 2021 revised that figure to 50.4% after including more data in their calculations.
